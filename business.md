Driftwood is a small-batch specialty coffee roaster in Burleigh Heads on the Gold Coast. We roast single-origin and blend coffees, sell whole beans online and by monthly subscription, run a cafe, and supply wholesale beans to a few local cafes. Open 7 days, cafe 6:30am to 2pm. Online orders ship Tuesdays and Thursdays, Australia-wide. We do not promise a delivery date, AusPost handles transit. Most customer emails are: where is my order, what is this month's subscription coffee, do you do wholesale, and brew advice. Voice: warm, knowledgeable, unpretentious; we talk like people, never corporate. Owner: Mia. Complaints, wholesale pricing, and anything sensitive go to Mia.
