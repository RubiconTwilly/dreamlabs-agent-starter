# Your First AI Employee (Dream Labs Plug & Play)

This repo is the entire brain of one AI agent that works for your business on a schedule. A few markdown files, that is it.

- `CLAUDE.md` - the rules every run follows (loads automatically)
- `business.md` - what your business is (comes written from your diagnostic)
- `agent.md` - the one job this agent does (your first agent, from the diagnostic)
- `brand-voice.md` - how you sound (comes written from your diagnostic, drawn from how you already talk online)
- `routine-prompt.md` - the text you paste into your Claude routine
- `INTEGRATIONS.md` - every key, and how to get it
- `learnings.md` - the agent's memory, it updates this itself
- `drafts/` - the agent's run log

It runs in DRAFT MODE: it prepares the work, you approve it. It never sends anything on its own.

Full setup is in the tutorial. Build time is about 30 minutes.
